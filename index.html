The FODMAP Diet

Information and Instruction

A commitment diet that will help you maintain a healthier digestive track.

Live pain free.

Welcome to the FODMAP diet. This is a diet for both weight loss and people who struggle with intestinal disorders and diseases. The most common disorder that gastroenterologists find is eased by this diet is Irritable Bowel Sydrome of IBS.

Avoid

Made to avoid foods that disrupt the gastro-intestinal track.

Enjoy

Made of foods that either don't effect you or help prevent any damage to your digestive track.

Why?

For people struggling with bowel disorders and disease to relieve pain.

If you stick with it...

The intestinal cramps will decrease as well as the contracting of abdominal muscles.

Make sure to take fiber supplements because you may not be getting the necessary amount from your food.

It can also increase the amount of energy and motivation you have by cutting out junk foods.
